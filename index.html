FOODBLOCK

First company introducing blockchain to food industry.

Meet Foodblock

Our goal is to give customers ability to track step by step history of grocery products.
We can achieve such possibilities by creating first blockchain tracking system worldwide.


Full history

Every step from supplier to market shelf is saved in chain.


100% reliable

Thanks to blockchain data stored in system can't be deleted or modified.


Available to everyone

You need only your smartphone to start scanning and checking products.

Get started

Step one:

Download Foodblock app for your smartphone. It's currently available for: Android and iOS.
Windows Phone coming soon!

Step two:

Scan a product.
It can be done by scanning QR code or touching NFC tag.

Step three:

Browse product history.
You will have access to full path of the product. From the supplier to the store shelf.
